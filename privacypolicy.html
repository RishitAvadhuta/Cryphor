Privacy Policy

In order to run the Cryphor game, we rely on Epic Online Services (EOS) to provide the necessary online services. EOS is a third-party service that may collect and process personal data in accordance with its own privacy policy. We do not have access to or control over the data collected by EOS, and we encourage you to review their privacy policy for more information on how they handle your data. Read it here.

While the game is open, the game accesses basic account details (e.g. Username) and your friends list in order to provide Peer-To-Peer (P2P) networking and multiplayer. We do not store any information.
